Our Team

We are based at Birkbeck, University of London, but make up a geographically distributed network of expert bookworms, specialist software developers, and typography enthusiasts.

Although we work remotely from across the UK and beyond, we’re united by our belief in the power of shared knowledge and our commitment to the open access revolution. We have a shared love of arts and culture, retro gaming, AI glitches, dreadful puns; we spend our days sharing ironic gratitude memes and working to make the world of academic publishing a better place.
